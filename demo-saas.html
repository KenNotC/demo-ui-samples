Getting started

  1. Verify your email — Check your inbox for the confirmation link.
  2. Create your workspace — Name it after your company or project.
  3. Invite your team — Add members from Settings → Team.
  4. Connect your first integration — See the Integrations section below.

Authentication

Your account supports email/password login and SSO. To enable SSO:

  1. Go to Settings → Security
  2. Select your identity provider (Okta, Google Workspace, Azure AD)
  3. Follow the provider-specific setup guide
  4. Test with a non-admin account before enforcing SSO for all users
Enforcing SSO will log out all existing sessions. Notify your team before enabling.
